    简介¶

    “Deep-space Positioning System” (DPS) which is a selfcontained instrument concept that would use in-situ observations to provide an automated and autonomous means of determining its position in the Solar System, and at least as important, its position relative to a target, whether that target is a planet, asteroid, moon or comet

    DPS不仅确定在太阳系内的位置还确定相对目标的位置（行星、小天体）

    要估计的参数还可能包括：加计零偏、相机焦距、目标星历误差、相机校正参数（具备在线校正功能）

    敏感器组成¶

    15kg， 40cmx15cmx15cm ![[Pasted image 20201204082628.png]]

    self-pointing narrow and wide-angle cameras 还具备单向的高精度无线电测距功能，依赖于深空原子钟DSAC

    还可以对artificial satellites人造卫星成像 #灵感

    集成了如下设备： 1. the miniature MRO OpNav telescope8 2. the Iris radio (which will fly on the INSPIRE and MARCO cubesat missions) – to provide computational power and one-way radio reception for DPS 3. the Mars2020 EECam CMOS 20Megapixel camera electronics and detectors 4. the Deep Impact AutoNav autonomous navigation system (with additional Stardust and Deep Space 1 flight heritage) 5. the MER X-band patch antenna (for the 1-way radio link navigation) 6. a command and control s/w suite that has been the automated robotic control language used on over a dozen of NASA’s missions, including the Spitzer space telescope and Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter.

    核心部件：Advanced Pointing and Imaging Camera (APIC)： obtain very high-resolution images of planetary surfaces while simultaneously determining the pointing from the camera to that surface, and in so doing, extracting information with regard to motions and deformations in planetary surfaces.

    **DPS FSW**组成：

    • The AutoNav autonomous and automated navigation system, developed for Deep Space 1, and flown additionally on Stardust and Deep Impact
    • The Virtual Machine Language (VML 3) developed originally for the SIRTF/Spitzer space telescope
    • the Iris plug-in application layer that hosts the IRIS software defined radio application as well as AutoNav.

    任务应用¶

    火星探测¶

    MRO开展了接近段的光学导航试验，利用optical navigation camera (ONC)技术对火星卫星的成像（相对于背景恒星）确定航天器相对火星的位置。

    在到达火星前3.3天到28天进行成像，得到了480幅图像。由于可观性不好，和地面站的多普勒测量进行了结合。指标精度为1km以内，实际精度优于指标精度。

    火星探测任务的导航思路：

    1. 地球离开段和返回段：对人造卫星成像，确定相对地球和太阳系的位置
    2. 在中端巡航段，对小行星成像
    3. 在接近段：对小行星和火星卫星成像
    4. 环绕段：对火星边缘和火星表面成像。

    optical -only navigation: #灵感 An optical-only solution should be possible even in the absence of a priori knowledge, by searching in succession for planets, moons, asteroids and, when applicable, limbs and surface features, and this optical-only solution can then be used to reduce the search space, in orientation and frequency, for ground transmitted navigation signals, allowing for an autonomous high-performance navigation capability even after a cold restart ^10cb8d 能够适应系统故障导致导航状态先验信息完全丧失的情况，通过连续搜索行星、行星卫星、小行星、甚至是局部特征实现自主导航。 ^a69a72
