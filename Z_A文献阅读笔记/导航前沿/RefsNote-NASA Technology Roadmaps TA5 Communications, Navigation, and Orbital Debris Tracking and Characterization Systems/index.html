    介绍和总结¶

    目标¶

    导航和授时的目标为：（page: TA5-4）

    • 更为精确的跟踪用于降低轨迹误差、降低EDL过程和交会对接过程的误差
    • 在不影响精度的前提下以更少的时间跟踪更多的目标
    • 自主自动轨迹和机动规划
    • 更为精确和稳定的授时基准用于更 高精度的导航和自主操作

    随着导航定位和授时技术的进步，航天器可以在太阳系内的任意地点自主导航

    先进导航的必要性和益处¶

    (page TA5-6) 1. 更为精确的定位可以获取更高质量的数据回报，比如高分辨率相机 2. 更为精确的定位是实现复杂任务的前提条件,比如高精度着陆、高精度编队飞行。 3. 降低任务成本：自主数据数据，无需地面参与 4. 降低载人任务的风险

    上面的可以总结为： 1. 降低成本 2. 促进复杂任务实施 3. 降低风险 4. 获取更多数据回报

    此外还可以有更多的商业回报

    导航定位和授时（技术分类5.4）¶

    PNT技术分为如下几类：

    • 时钟保持系统和架构：超高精度时间和稳定频率的时间和频率信号
    • 在轨自主导航和机动：降低对地面站的依赖性
    • 敏感器和视觉处理系统：光学敏感器硬件（lidar，视觉、红外等）、雷达敏感器、无线电，制导算法，敏感器测量数据处理算法、视觉硬件和软件、场景感知显示
    • 相对和接近导航：Technologies include those that enable the ability to perform multi-platform relative navigation (such as determine relative position, relative velocity, and relative attitude or pose), which directly supports cooperative and collaborative space platform operations.
    • 自主精确编队飞行：Technologies include differential (relative) navigation, sensors and vision processing systems, space clocks and time or frequency distribution systems, onboard system navigation, and autonomous orbit and attitude maneuvering.
    • 自主接近和着陆：Technologies to perform safe and controlled precision landings on or contacts with any solid body in the solar system.

    TA5.4.1 时间保持和分发架构¶

    提高导航能力的基本是提高星上时钟的精度和稳定性

    Deep-Space Atomic Clock (DSAC, 深空原子钟)：这种时钟放航天器上，由于其足够精确，因此单向传输即可实现测距（地面站）；此外，不同航天器的通信测量也能实现类GPS网络。

    based on trapped mercury ions ina vacuum tube 基于真空管中捕获的汞离子； 面临的挑战：降低尺寸、重量和功耗（SWaP)，降低研制成本。

    高精度时钟的应用：PNT, 基础物理，时间和频率计量学，大地测量学和重量分析， 和超高分辨率VLBI科学应用

    高级时间保持系统**基于高度稳定的石英晶体谐振器或测量原子跃迁的技术建立计时系统的频率标准，包括光学钟; **利用石英晶体谐振器时钟的主要挑战：降低其对在热环境、磁和电场，引力和电离辐射效应等的在轨敏感性； **基于原子的太空钟的主要技术**挑战在降低其复杂性和成本的同时保持其高端性能。

    **常见的技术挑战**包括减少整体计时系统SWaP资源需求；防辐射，低噪声时钟读出电子设备；处理时钟测量并估计或传播生成时间的计时模型（用于产生时间和频率信号输出）的软件算法。

    开发阶段： - 系统开发，例如时间和频率分配架构，技术和 方法论；系统误差和不确定性建模； - 硬件组件开发，例如影响时间/的射频或光通信设备频率转移； - 软件组件的开发： 滤波算法，以传播常见的时间和频率参考的实时估计

    长期目标：太阳系内的纳秒级时间传输

    TA 5.4.2 在轨自主导航和机动¶

    应用实例¶

    1. Orion: 敏感器和算法、飞行软件：鲁棒性好、稳定。能够和太阳系内的任务目标进行交会对接，而不需要地面支持。
    2. Origins Spectral Interpretation Resource Identification Security – Regolith Explorer (OSIRIS-Rex) 小行星采样：在接近小行星表面附件时的TAG阶段，利用3D LIDAR传感器执行实时在轨导航功能，更新先验地面确定的CheckPoint和MatchPoint推进机动。3D LIDAR还设计为在TAG阶段用作相对导航传感器，以确保航天器在既定的射程和射程速率安全走廊内飞行。

    自主应用：¶

    servicing and assembly, sample return, formation flying,natural body fly-around, non-cooperative object capture, and pinpoint landing 在轨服务和装配、采样返回、编队飞行、绕飞、非合作目标捕获、精确着陆

    需要发展的技术¶

    • 自主导航架构和技术 Autonomous navigation system architectures and techniques;
    • 自主导航规划和优化算法, 以及高可靠的故障管理 Autonomous navigational planning and optimization algorithms, including highly-reliable approaches for fault management;
    • 自主导航敏感器 Sensors for onboard autonomous navigation;
    • 自主导航滤波算法 Navigation filter algorithms;
    • 在轨机动规划执行算法Onboard maneuver planning and sequencing algorithms;
    • 容忍故障的姿态控制系统以能够自主的进行指向，实施观测Fault-tolerant attitude control systems for autonomously orienting the platform
    • 自主导航验证技术Efficient autonomous navigation system verification and validation methodologies;
    • 硬件在回路的地面试验设备 test bed
    • 在轨试验设备。

    实现目标¶

    • 高级层面：能够监测和处理软硬件故障而不影响任务成功或安全
    • 导航层面：具备FDIR功能，能够监测敏感器层面的故障和算法故障 包括： 硬件级自检，测量数据品质检测，敏感器测量数据对比，一致性检验等
    • 算法层面：同一套软件有多个实例，每个实例对应不同的敏感器输入 （和前后台滤波有点像) #灵感
    • 敏感器层面： 敏感器冗余

    The FDIR responsibilities for dissimilar sensors are exceedingly complex and need to be addressed.

    支持导航陆标的导航架构¶

    System architectures and supporting technologies for navigational beacons and optical targets that aid spacecraft approach and landing are needed. The navigational beacons and targets can be deployed on early exploration satellites, landers, and rovers. Navigational beacons can be integrated into the precursor satellites, which will be used to map and study planet and NEA surface characteristics. These orbiting beacons can be part of a navigation constellation for improved vehicle positioning during the approach and landing mission phase. Likewise, body based beacon landing systems derived from GPS pseudolite technologies and landing optical targets can be used for accurate landing systems. A pre-landing mission can deploy three to five beacons or targets. These beacons and targets can land in rough, unknown locations using air bags or parachutes. Through repeated observation, beacons’ and targets’ positions can be precisely determined. With knowledge of beacon and target location, future landing craft can use precise timing beacons and optical targets for improved navigation accuracy similar to GPS pseudolites and ISS proximity and docking targets.

    TA 5.6.3 中子星导航¶

    Neutrino-based navigation and tracking: 目的：无法保证视线方向的信号传输的情况下实现宇宙中的位置和姿态确定。 利用angle of arrival （AoA）实现位置和姿态确定。挑战：Neutrino generator and detector sizes and event detection rates are currently orders of magnitude removed from practicality for either deep-space communications or navigation.

    Neutrino-Based Navigation and Tracking

    since neutrinos can travel through most matter, they could be used for long-distance signaling when line-of-sight cannot be guaranteed. A possible ranging method using neutrinos is to modulate the neutrino output from a nuclear reactor or particle accelerator on a spacecraft and detect this output at an Earth base station. The modulation would encode timing information from an atomic clock synchronized to Earth, which can be used to calculate range.

    技术挑战¶

    Current neutrino detectors have fairly high accuracy in estimating angle of arrival, but they are massive and have very low rates of detection. Hence, the size and power consumption of neutrino detectors need to be significantly reduced for practical in-space navigation applications. The objective is to achieve 100- to 1,000-fold improvement in detector SWaP. Detection of neutrinos currently requires massive detectors made of thousands of tons of liquid buried in the ground. Neutrino generator and detector sizes and event detection rates are currently orders of magnitude removed from practicality for either deep-space communications or navigation.
