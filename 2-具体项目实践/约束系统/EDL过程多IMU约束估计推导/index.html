    简单约束¶

    2套IMU¶

    令\(a_1,a_2\)为纯惯外推得到的惯性位置， 则泛函指标可以写作 $$ \begin{matrix} J = \frac{1}{2}(r_1-a_1)T(r_1-a_1)+\frac{1}{2}(r_2-a_2)T(r_2-a_2)+\frac{1}{2}\lambda( |r_1-r_2|2-c2 ) \end{matrix} $$ 对未知状态求偏导可以得到： $$ \begin{matrix}\label{e:Jpartial-1} \frac{\partial J}{\partial r_1} &= (r_1-a_1) + \lambda (r_1-r_2) =0\ \frac{\partial J}{\partial r_2} &= (r_2-a_2) - \lambda (r_1-r_2) =0\ \frac{\partial J}{\partial \lambda} &= |r_1-r_2|2-c2 =0 \end{matrix} $$ 由式\(\ref{e:Jpartial-1}\)的第1和第2式可以求得 $$ \begin{matrix}\label{e:r1r2mins-1} r_1 = \frac{\lambda a_2 + (1+\lambda)a_1}{2\lambda+1}\ r_2 = \frac{\lambda a_1 + (1+\lambda)a_2}{2\lambda+1}\ r_1-r_2 = \frac{1}{1+2\lambda}(a_1-a_2) \end{matrix} $$ 将上式代入到式\(\ref{e:Jpartial-1}\)的第3式可以求得： $$ \begin{matrix}\label{e:sol-lambda-1} \lambda = \pm \frac{|a_1-a_2|}{2 c}-\frac{1}{2} \end{matrix} $$ 将\(\ref{e:sol-lambda}\)代入到\(\ref{e:r1r2mins}\)即可求得\(r_1,r_2\).

    4 IMU¶

    4套IMU能够提炼的约束有几个？ 还有一个约束就是123构成的平面，4在平面的一侧，而不是另外一侧。相对于右手定则 （\(r_{12},r_{13},r_{14}\), \(r_{14}^T(r_{12}\times r_{13})\)=常数, 有点复杂，暂时不考虑）

    令\(a_1,a_2,a_3,a_4\)为纯惯外推得到的惯性位置， 则泛函指标可以写作 $$ \begin{matrix} J = \frac{1}{2}(r_1-a_1)T(r_1-a_1)+\frac{1}{2}(r_2-a_2)T(r_2-a_2)\ +\frac{1}{2}(r_3-a_3)T(r_3-a_3)+\frac{1}{2}(r_4-a_4)T(r_4-a_4)\ +\frac{1}{2}\lambda_{12}( |r_1-r_2|2-c_{12}2 ) \ +\frac{1}{2}\lambda_{13}( |r_1-r_3|2-c_{13}2 ) \ +\frac{1}{2}\lambda_{23}( |r_2-r_3|2-c_{23}2 ) \ +\frac{1}{2}\lambda_{14}( |r_1-r_4|2-c_{14}2 ) \ +\frac{1}{2}\lambda_{24}( |r_2-r_4|2-c_{24}2 ) \ +\frac{1}{2}\lambda_{34}( |r_3-r_4|2-c_{34}2 ) \end{matrix} $$ 对未知状态求偏导可以得到： $$ \begin{matrix}\label{e:Jpartial1234-1} \frac{\partial J}{\partial r_1} &= (r_1-a_1) + \lambda_{12} (r_1-r_2) + \lambda_{13} (r_1-r_3)+\lambda_{14} (r_1-r_4) =0\ \frac{\partial J}{\partial r_2} &= (r_2-a_2) - \lambda_{12} (r_1-r_2) +\lambda_{23}(r_2-r_3)+\lambda_{24}(r_2-r_4)=0\ \frac{\partial J}{\partial r_3} &= (r_3-a_3) - \lambda_{13} (r_1-r_3) -\lambda_{23}(r_2-r_3)+\lambda_{34}(r_3-r_4)=0\ \frac{\partial J}{\partial r_4} &= (r_4-a_4) - \lambda_{14} (r_1-r_4) -\lambda_{24}(r_2-r_4)-\lambda_{34}(r_3-r_4)=0 \ \frac{\partial J}{\partial \lambda_{12}} &= |r_1-r_2|2-c_{12}2 =0\ \frac{\partial J}{\partial \lambda_{23}} &= |r_2-r_3|2-c_{23}2 =0\ \frac{\partial J}{\partial \lambda_{13}} &= |r_1-r_3|2-c_{13}2 =0\ \frac{\partial J}{\partial \lambda_{14}} &= |r_1-r_4|2-c_{14}2 =0\ \frac{\partial J}{\partial \lambda_{24}} &= |r_2-r_4|2-c_{24}2 =0\ \frac{\partial J}{\partial \lambda_{34}} &= |r_3-r_4|2-c_{34}2 =0 \end{matrix} $$ 由式\(\ref{e:Jpartial}\)的第1和第4式可以得到 $$ \begin{matrix} \Lambda x = \tilde{a} \end{matrix} $$ 其中, \(x=[r_1^T,r_2^T,r_3^T,r_4^T]^T\), \(\tilde{a}=[a_1^T,a_2^T,a_3^T,a_4^T]^T\) $$ \begin{matrix} \Lambda = \left[\begin{array}{ccc} 1+\lambda_{12}+\lambda_{13}+\lambda_{14} & -\lambda_{12} & -\lambda_{13} &-\lambda_{14}\ -\lambda_{12} & 1+\lambda_{12}+\lambda_{23}+\lambda_{24} & -\lambda_{23} &-\lambda_{24}\ -\lambda_{13} &-\lambda_{23} & 1+\lambda_{13}+\lambda_{23}+\lambda_{34} &-\lambda_{34}\ -\lambda_{14} &-\lambda_{24} & -\lambda_{34} & 1+\lambda_{14}+\lambda_{24}+\lambda_{34} \end{array} \right] \end{matrix} $$ 因此可以求的 $$ \begin{matrix}\label{e:sol-x1-1} x=\Lambda^{-1}\tilde{a} \end{matrix} $$

    令\(y=[r_{12}^T,r_{23}^T,r_{13}^T,r_{14}^T,r_{24}^T,r_{34}^T]^T\), 其中\(r_{ij}=r_{i}-r_{j}\).则有 $$ \begin{matrix}\label{e:rel-xy-1} \Gamma x = y \end{matrix} $$ 其中： $$ \begin{matrix} \Gamma = \left[\begin{array}{ccc} I & -I & 0 & 0 \ 0& I & -I & 0 \ I & 0& -I & 0 \ I & 0& 0 & -I \ 0 & I& 0 & -I \ 0 &0 & I& -I \end{array} \right] \end{matrix} $$ 将式\(\ref{e:sol-x1-1}\)代入到式\(\ref{e:rel-xy-1}\)中可以得到 $$ \begin{matrix}\label{e:lambda-eqn-1} \Gamma\Lambda^{-1}\tilde{a} = y \end{matrix} $$ 相应的约束为 $$ \begin{matrix}\label{e:y-constraints-1} |y|i = d \quad i=1,2,3,4,5,6 \end{matrix} $$ 其中\(d=[c_{12},c_{23},c_{13},c_{14},c_{24},c_{34}]^T\).

    姿态约束¶

    姿态四元数乘积¶

    几个关于四元数的等式 $$ \begin{matrix} \mathbf{q}1\otimes q_2 &= [\Psi(\mathbf{q}_1) \mathbf{q}_1]\mathbf{q}_2 \ &= [\Xi[\bm{q}_2] \bm{q}_2] \mathbf{q}_1 \end{matrix} $$ 其中： $$ \begin{matrix} \Xi(\bm{q}) = \left[\begin{matrix} qI_3 + [\bm{q}v\times] \ -\bm{q}_v^T \end{matrix} \right] \ \Psi(\bm{q}) = \left[\begin{matrix} qI_3 - [\bm{q}_v\times] \ -\bm{q}_v^T \end{matrix} \right] \end{matrix} $$

    定义算子:\(\bm{L}(\bm{q})=\bm{q}_v\)，则有 $$ \begin{matrix} \bm{L}(\mathbf{q}1\otimes q_2) &=& \bm{L} ( \Psi(\mathbf{q}_1)\bm{q} + \bm{q}1q ) \ &=& (q_{1s} -[\bm{q}{1v}\times]) \bm{q} + \bm{q}{1v}q \end{matrix} $$ 从而有： $$ \begin{matrix} \bm{L}(\bar{\mathbf{q}}1^{-1}\otimes q_1) &=& (\bar{q} +[\bar{\bm{q}}{1v}\times]) \bm{q} - \bar{\bm{q}}{1v}q \ &\equiv& A_{1}\bm{q}{1v} + bq_{1s} \end{matrix} $$ 从而有 $$ \begin{matrix} J_1 \equiv \frac{1}{2}\bm{L}{T}(\bar{\mathbf{q}}_1\otimes q_1)\bm{L}(\bar{\mathbf{q}}1^{-1}\otimes q_1) = \frac{1}{2} \bm{q}TA_{1}TA_{1} \bm{q}{1v} +\frac{1}{2} q2\bm{b}_{1}T\bm{b}1 + q\bm{b}1^T A\bm{q}{1v} \end{matrix} $$ 从而有： $$ \begin{matrix} \frac{\partial J_1}{\partial \bm{q}} &=& A_{1}^TA_{1} \bm{q}{1v} + qA_{1}^T \bm{b}1 \ \frac{\partial J_1}{\partial \bm{q}} &=& {b}{1}^T\bm{b}_1 q + \bm{b}1^T A\bm{q}_{1v} \end{matrix} $$

    同理， $$ \begin{matrix} \bm{L}(\bar{\mathbf{q}}2^{-1}\otimes q_2) &=& (\bar{q} +[\bar{\bm{q}}{2v}\times]) \bm{q} - \bar{\bm{q}}{2v}q \ &\equiv& A_{2}\bm{q}{2v} + bq_{2s} \end{matrix} $$ 进而有 $$ \begin{matrix} \frac{\partial J_2}{\partial \bm{q}{2v}} &=& A^TA_{2} \bm{q}{2v} + qA_{2}^T \bm{b}2 \ \frac{\partial J_2}{\partial \bm{q}} &=& {b}{2}^T\bm{b}_2 q + \bm{b}2^T A\bm{q}_{2v} \end{matrix} $$

    此外，如果\(\bm{q}_1\)对应\(\mathcal{F}_i\)到135imu的姿态矩阵，\(\bm{q}_2\)对应 \(\mathcal{F}_i\)到246imu的姿态矩阵，则有 $$ \begin{matrix} \frac{\partial (q_1-\bar{q}_3\otimes q_2)}{q_1} = I \ \frac{\partial (q_1-\bar{q}_3\otimes q_2)}{q_2} = -[\Psi(\bar{\mathbf{q}}_3) \bar{\mathbf{q}}_3] \ \end{matrix} $$ 其中\(\bar{\bm{q}}_3\)为246imu到135imu的姿态矩阵。

    姿态约束估计¶

    约束泛函指标可以写作 $$ \begin{matrix} J = J_1 + J_2 + \frac{1}{2}\lambda_1(\bm{q}_1^T\bm{q}_1-1) +\frac{1}{2} \lambda_2(\bm{q}_2^T\bm{q}_2-1) + \bm{\lambda}_3^T (q_1-\bar{q}_3\otimes q_2) \end{matrix} $$

    \(\lambda_3\)还可以用norm范数来约束，但是求偏导有些麻烦。

    {#eq:a3} [@eq:a3] 最优必要条件为 $$ \begin{matrix} \frac{\partial J}{\partial q_1} &=& \bm{C}_1 \bm{q}_1 + \lambda_1\bm{q}_1 +\bm{\lambda}_3 =0\ \frac{\partial J}{\partial q_2} &=& \bm{C}_2 \bm{q}_1 + \lambda_2\bm{q}_2 - \left([\Psi(\bar{\mathbf{q}}_3) \bar{\mathbf{q}}_3]\right)^T \bm{\lambda}_3=0\ \bm{q}_1^T\bm{q}_1-1 &=& 0 \ \bm{q}_2^T\bm{q}_2-1 &=& 0 \ q_1-\bar{q}_3\otimes q_2&=&\bm{0} \end{matrix} $$

    其中 $$ \begin{matrix} \bm{C}_1 = \left[\begin{matrix} \bm{A}_1^T\bm{A}_1 \quad \bm{A}_1\bm{b}_1 \ \bm{b}_1^T\bm{A}_1 \quad \bm{b}_1^T\bm{b}_1 \end{matrix} \right] \end{matrix} $$ 由上式前2式可得 $$ \begin{matrix} \bm{q}_1 = -(\bm{C}_1 + \lambda_1 I)^{-1}\bm{\lambda}_3 \ \bm{q}_2 = (\bm{C}_2 + \lambda_2 I)^{-1}\bm{D}\bm{\lambda}_3 \end{matrix} $$ 再代入到后3式进行牛顿迭代法求解得到\(\lambda_1\),\(\lambda_2\)和\(\bm{\lambda}_3\)
