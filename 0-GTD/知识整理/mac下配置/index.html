    mac下matlab中文乱码¶

    首先输入 

        feature('locale')

ans = 

  struct with fields:

               ctype: 'en_US_POSIX.UTF-8'
             collate: 'en_US_POSIX.UTF-8'
                time: 'en_US_POSIX.UTF-8'
             numeric: 'en_US_POSIX.UTF-8'
            monetary: 'en_US_POSIX.UTF-8'
            messages: 'en_US.UTF-8'
            encoding: 'UTF-8'
    terminalEncoding: 'UTF-8'
         jvmEncoding: 'UTF-8'
              status: 'MathWorks locale management system initialized.'
             warning: 'Invalid locale name.'

    打开matlab安装路径bin\lcdata_utf8.xml,搜索en_US_POSIX将其编码换成下面的： 

        <locale name="en_US_POSIX" encoding="GB18030" xpg_name="GB18030">
        <alias name="C"/>
        <alias name="POSIX"/>
    </locale>
    然后将lcdata_utf8.xml重命名为lcdata.xml重启matlab即可。
