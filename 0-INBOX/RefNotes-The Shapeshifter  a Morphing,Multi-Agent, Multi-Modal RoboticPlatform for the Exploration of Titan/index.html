    概述¶

    能滚、能飞、能游泳

    仿真环境： ROS + Gazebo

    每个单元称为Cobots, 本质上是无人机，

    重量 1.1kg
    功耗 31
    体积 0.03m^3

    Cobots携带的设备：

    • 采样工具sample collecting tool
    • IMU
    • stereo相机
    • radiation monitor
    • thermocouple

    介绍¶

    现有探测方式的不足¶

    现有单一的探测方式，经常用于探测特殊的地形，对于特殊的地形，高风险和高回报的探测场景，不能保证足够的失败裕度。不可预测且不断变化的环境[23]此外，还指出了对灵活，冗余，多用途移动解决方案的需求，这些解决方案是能够跨域高效移动。

    新提出的多智能体机器人¶

    cobot，每个cobot有无人机和半圆锥外壳组成，能够飞、滚以及游泳。并利用programmable magnets (PM) based on permanent electro-magnet (PEM)对多个cobot进行对接。

    programmable polymagnets 见：M. R. Sullivan, S. J. Pirotta, V. Chernenko, G. Wu, G. Balasubramanium, S. Z. Hua, and H. D. Chopra, “Magnetic mosaics in crystalline tiles: The novel concept of polymagnets,” *International Journal of Applied Electromagnetics* *and Mechanics*, vol. 22, no. 1, 2, pp. 11–23, 2005

    每个cobot能够进行基于视觉的SLAM

    基站(HOME-BASE)的功能：

    • 执行所必需的仪器科学测量并分析Cobots（例如质谱仪）收集的样品，
    • 基于放射性同位素的电力系统（RPS），例如多任务放射性同位素热电发生器（MMRTG）[34]或模块化斯特林放射性同位素发生器（MSRG），为家庭基地本身提供电源并为Cobot的电池充电
    • 托管与地球建立通信链路所需的设备

    样机设计¶

    组成：

    four 6-inch propellers, EMax 2300 kV brushless-DC motors, and a three-cell 2200 mAh battery

    在轨电力和IMU由PX4 flight stack提供

    在轨计算由qualcomm snapdragon 开发板提供（包含2.1ghz 4核CPU, 相机，IMU ），还有无线连接功能。

    NASA的火星直升机Mars helicopter technology demonstrator采用的也是qualcomm snapdragon开发板

    定位算法：¶

    Large-scale Autonomous Mapping and Positioning (LAMP) is our proposed framework to meet the multi-agent localization and mapping requirements of the mission. LAMP is based on Distributed Pose Graph Optimization (DPGO)

    状态估计：多模式状态估计（Visual, Thermal, Laser, Inertial ）multiple parallel estimators

    规划和长期操作¶

    Cobots will employ a holistic framework [72], [73], which leverages combined mapping, obstacle avoidance, and 3D trajectory generation to guarantee performance levels not achievable by conventional, de-coupled approaches.

    [72] B. J. Morrell, G. E. Chamitoff, D. J. Kuether, M. Coen, and P. Gibbens, “Integration of 3d slam, rigid body landmarks and 3d path planning,” in AIAA SPACE 2016, 2016, p. 5411. [73] B. Morrell, “Enhancing 3d autonomous navigation through obstacle fields: Homogeneous localisation and mapping, with obstacle-aware trajectory optimisation,” 2018.

    识别感兴趣点¶

    We are currently exploring machine learning based semantic understanding integrated within the Distributed Pose Graph Optimization framework to provide the global location of specific topological or geological features in the constructed map, such as canyons, rivers, lakes, or junctions in cave networks.

    Learning based approaches will additionally allow for automatic detection of novel science opportunities, such as geological features [75], which will be used to autonomously define tasks for the mission.

    [75] H. R. Kerner, D. F. Wellington, K. L. Wagstaff, J. F. Bell, and H. B. Amor, “Novelty detection for multispectral images with application to planetary exploration,” 2019.

    轨迹生成¶

    The approach proposed in [81], [82], [83] will allow to generate a dynamically-feasible motion plan subject to a constraint on collision probability, and will allow to best tradeoff the competing objectives of performance and safety, for example depending on the level of risk that the Shapeshifter mission is able to accept at its different stages. The method described in [84] will instead guarantee that the Shapeshifter will always operate at the most energy-efficient velocity, regardless of unexpected factors such as actuator degradation or payloads, by finding online the velocity which maximizes the range or the endurance of the robot.
